My Rig v1.0

This was my first ever computer build, POSTing for the first time. The build had an Intel i7-4790k with 120mm water cooler and 8GB of RAM on an ASUS Maximus VII Hero Motherboard. It had a 1TB WD Blue HDD and a 750W PSU all in a Fractal Design Define R4. Later, I added a 120GB SSD boot drive and an MSI GTX 660 pixel pusher.


My Rig v2.0

For v2.0, I wasn't messing around. I used the same Intel i7-4790k as before, now custom water cooled. The water loop also contains a EVGA GTX 970 Superclocked pixel pusher with an EK Water Block. RAM went up to 16GB, and all of it's on an ASRock Z97M Motherboard. It's powered by a 500W modular passive power supply, and has the same 120GB SSD boot drive. The water loop consists of a Swiftech H220X mounted backwards, the extra 120mm radiator on the bottom, the GTX 970's EK waterblock and chrome compression fittings all around. All of this is packed into a Bitfenix Prodigy M

212cc Mini Bike

This is the crazy mini bike I built. It's a kit frame with a Harbor Freight Predator 212 engine driving it. The 212 has gotten a full stage 1 kit including carb jet, emulsion tube, intake, exhaust and 8 degrees of timing advance. The 212 makes a little over 7.5 HP now. I reduced the gearing in the back to help stop the wheelies, and now it'll do ~35 mph.
